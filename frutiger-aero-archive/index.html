Plugins 🧩

Add retro themes and mods for games and apps.

  • Roblox Themes: .roblokis files (requires Roblokis extension)
  • Minecraft Contents: Mods, resource packs, modpacks, and shaders (give credits!)
  • Scratch Themes: userstyles.world, requires Stylus Extension
  • 3rd Party PC Apps: RetroBar, OpenShell, etc. ⚠️ Use with caution!
